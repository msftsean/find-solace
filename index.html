The Basics

What is AI, really?

Forget everything you've seen in the movies.

Think of AI as a very fast research assistant that never gets tired. It can read thousands of pages in seconds, spot patterns you'd miss, and summarize things in plain language.

It's not magic and it's not a robot. It's software — like the spell-check you already use, but much more capable. When you ask it a question, it draws on everything it's learned to give you a helpful answer.

The tools we'll show you aren't science experiments. They're practical apps you can use today — for things like figuring out your taxes, planning a trip, or writing a better email.

You don't need to understand how it works to benefit from it. You don't understand how your car engine works either — and that's fine.
