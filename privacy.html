Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What We Collect

  • Email address — only if you sign up for our AI Starter Kit.
  • Anonymous usage analytics via Microsoft Application Insights (page views, session duration, browser/OS). No personal data is collected.

How We Use It

  • Email: To send you occasional, practical AI tips. That's it.
  • Analytics: To understand which content is helpful and improve the site.

What We Don't Do

  • We never sell your information.
  • We never share your email with third parties.
  • We never send spam.
  • We don't use tracking cookies — analytics are cookie-free.

Your Control

  • You can decline analytics via the consent banner on any page.
  • If your browser sends a Do Not Track signal, we respect it automatically.
  • To unsubscribe, reply to any email or contact us.

Data Storage

  • Emails are stored securely in Azure Table Storage (Microsoft cloud).
  • Analytics data is processed by Microsoft Application Insights.
  • All data stays within Microsoft's Azure infrastructure.
